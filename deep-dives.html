QP Deep Dives

Longer follow-ups. More context. More signal.

A podcast-style extension of QP Thoughts for ideas that deserve a slower and deeper treatment.

What it is

Where the short thought becomes the deeper conversation.

QP Thoughts are the sharp written core. QP Deep Dives are the follow-up: longer, calmer, and more layered.

Some ideas need more room. More examples. More explanation. More connection to the wider pattern. That is what this section is for.

Format

Think of it as a podcast-style continuation of the written work: a deeper walk through the same themes, with more time to unfold them properly.

How it connects

  • `QP Thoughts` introduce the idea clearly and briefly.
  • `QP Deep Dives` take that same idea further with more depth and context.
  • `Books` hold the more complete and lasting body of work.

First Deep Dives are being shaped now.

The format is already defined: thoughtful, calm, and more developed than the short written pieces. This page will become the home for those episodes as they are published.
