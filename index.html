Episodes about calm work, modern life, incentives, and the kind of ideas that usually stay off the timeline.

Episode 01

Noise Looks Productive

Most people confuse activity with progress. In this episode, we explore how meetings, emails, and constant notifications create an illusion of work while stalling meaningful results. Learn why real progress only happens when you stop the cycle of reaction and start the process of deliberate thinking.

Episode 02

Incentives Shape Behaviour

Rules rarely change human behaviour; incentives do. This episode dives into the architecture of systems and explains why people naturally gravitate toward what is rewarded. If a system produces poor outcomes, we must stop blaming the people and start auditing the incentives that drive them.
