Quiet Idea #02 Incentives Shape Behaviour

Text...

Convenience often hides dependency

The easier a system feels, the less people usually ask what they are giving away in return.

Modern life rewards reaction more than reflection

Fast responses are visible. Careful thought is quieter, which is why it often gets ignored until it proves right.

Some people don’t want clarity. They want comfort.

Clarity creates responsibility. Comfort delays it. A lot of modern confusion survives because it feels easier than truth.
