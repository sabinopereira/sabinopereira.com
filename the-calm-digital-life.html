About the book

The digital world moves quickly, and scams often rely on pressure, confusion, and false urgency. This book is built to slow that moment down.

Instead of technical jargon, it offers clear habits and practical judgment so readers can reduce risk, protect their money, and feel more confident using digital tools.

What readers will get

  • Simple ways to spot pressure tactics before making a mistake.
  • Calmer decision habits for money, messages, calls, and online requests.
  • Clear principles for staying in control without needing technical expertise.

Who it is for

  • Adults over 60 who want more confidence online.
  • Anyone who wants to avoid scams without becoming overly fearful of technology.
  • Families looking for a practical resource they can share with parents or relatives.
