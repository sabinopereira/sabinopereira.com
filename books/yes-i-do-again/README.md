# Yes, I Do... Again.

English-language speculative relationship novel by Sabino Pereira.

## Editorial decisions

- Language: US English
- Setting: Lisbon, Portugal
- Main characters: Tomás, Leonor, and their son, Martim
- Form: continuous novelistic prose, not verse, screenplay beats, or line-fragment prose
- Dialogue: standard American double quotation marks
- Tone: intimate, adult, restrained, humane; no villain and no easy moral answer
- Title: *Yes, I Do... Again.*
- No tagline
