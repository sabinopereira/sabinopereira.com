## Short description

A premium digital book about survival, family, pressure, silence, love, loss—and

## Product description

Some people enter the game protected. Others begin with their backs against a
wall.

*Chess on the Block — Director's Cut* uses the pieces on a chessboard to explore
the invisible strategies people learn in families, neighborhoods, relationships,
and rooms where survival often speaks louder than healing.

The pawn carries what nobody sees. The knight finds a path where no straight road
exists. The bishop reads what remains unspoken. The rook holds the line. The queen
keeps everyone moving. The king learns that command is not control.

This revised edition presents the complete manuscript in polished prose while
preserving the spoken-word rhythm where the story needs to breathe.
