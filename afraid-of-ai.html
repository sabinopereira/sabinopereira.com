Series thesis

The real pressure is clarity.

Artificial intelligence is not making every profession irrelevant. But it is forcing many of them to become clearer. When information becomes easier to access, compare, and question, the old value of confusion starts to weaken.

What remains is the harder question: in this profession, what was real value and what was only friction, opacity, or control?

How to use this page

Choose the field closest to your work and notice the pattern: AI does not only automate tasks. It exposes where value was hidden behind delay, jargon, access, or confusion.
