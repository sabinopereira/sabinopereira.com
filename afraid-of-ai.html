QP Thought Series

Afraid of AI

AI does not remove the human role. It removes the fog around it.

A 10-part mini-series on what happens when asymmetry, opacity, and managed perception start to weaken across different professions.

10 chapters AI Clarity Professions April 10, 2026

Series Thesis

The real pressure is clarity.

Artificial intelligence is not forcing every profession into irrelevance. But it is forcing many of them into clarity. As information becomes easier to access, compare, and question, the old value of confusion begins to weaken.

What remains is the harder question: what in this profession was real value, and what was only friction, opacity, or control?

How to read it

Start at chapter one and move through the series, or jump directly into the profession that interests you most. Each chapter stands alone, but together they follow the same line.
