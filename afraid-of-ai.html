Series thesis

The real pressure is clarity.

Artificial intelligence is not forcing every profession into irrelevance. But it is forcing many of them into clarity. As information becomes easier to access, compare, and question, the old value of confusion begins to weaken.

What remains is the harder question: what in this profession was real value, and what was only friction, opacity, or control?

How to use this hub

Pick the field closest to your work, then notice the pattern: AI does not simply automate tasks. It exposes where value was hidden behind delay, jargon, access, or confusion.
