ABOUT

Sabino Pereira

Writing, books, Quiet Power Thoughts, and selected work.

Quiet Power sits at the centre of the work: a system for calm thinking, focused attention, and steadier action under pressure.

Beyond that, I write about modern life in different forms: books, short thoughts, observations, and projects that needed a clear shape.

I also work through design, objects, and digital spaces. For me, writing and design come from the same impulse: making something clear out of something messy.
