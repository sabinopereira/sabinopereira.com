About

I write about how people actually behave.

My work looks at money, pressure, discipline, technology, and everyday decisions. Not as theory, but as lived behaviour: what people say, what they avoid, what they repeat, and what pressure reveals.

Sometimes that becomes a short book. Sometimes an observation. Sometimes a simple tool. The aim is clear work with a real reason to exist.

This site

A personal platform for books, observations, and tools.

The site is designed to hold the different forms of the work together without confusion: short books, observations, practical tools, and occasional audio or video extensions. Different formats, one voice.

Recurring themes

  • Human behaviour under pressure
  • Money, excuses, discipline, and everyday decisions
  • Technology, AI, systems, and digital change
  • Contradiction, standards, avoidance, and structure

Books

Short books with bite.

Books are where a behaviour or pressure point gets enough space to become sharp.

Observations

Observations with signal.

Essays and reflections that stay compact but still carry an idea cleanly.
