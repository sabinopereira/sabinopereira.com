About

I make work about what people feel and reveal.

Sabino Pereira is the author name. Reira Bin is the music identity. Together they hold books, songs, observations, release pages, and artwork shaped by faith, memory, pressure, technology, love, and quiet power.

The work moves between direct reflection and emotional atmosphere: short books, essays, music releases, and useful entry points for people who want clarity without noise.

This site

A home for the different forms of the work.

Music, books, series, release pages, and links now sit under one cleaner language. The aim is simple: make the work easy to enter, easy to understand, and easy to follow.

Recurring themes

  • Faith, prayer, healing, love, and home
  • Human behaviour under pressure
  • Technology, AI, systems, and digital change
  • Memory, timing, silence, and quiet strength
