About

I write books, explore modern life, and build useful digital things.

Writing is how I make something clear out of something noisy. Sometimes that becomes a book. Sometimes a short piece. Sometimes a tool. Sometimes a story told out loud.

What matters to me is not producing more noise. It is building work with shape, thought, and a reason to exist.

This site

An author space, a living archive, and a digital lab.

The site is designed to hold the different forms of the work together without confusion: books, thoughts, tools, and occasional audio or video extensions. Different mediums, one voice.

Recurring themes

  • Modern life and how it shapes people
  • Calm thinking under pressure
  • AI, systems, and digital change
  • Behaviour, contradiction, standards, and structure
