About

I write about modern society, incentives and the hidden systems shaping human behavior.

My professional background is related to compliance and financial crime prevention, where observing complex financial systems and incentives is part of the daily work.

Earlier in my career I worked in banking operations, including customer support and telemarketing, which offered a different perspective on how large organizations function from the inside.

Over time, that experience expanded into writing about broader systems — how people think, how institutions behave, and how modern culture shapes individual decisions.

Alongside my professional work, I explore independent publishing, digital design and small creative experiments.

This website is a place to publish ideas, books and independent projects.
