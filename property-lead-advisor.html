Property Lead Advisor

Qualify better, follow up better, and move the right leads forward.

A practical commercial assistant for real estate professionals who need clearer qualification, stronger follow-up, CRM-ready notes, and better next steps across different lead types.

Open app See how it works

What it is

A practical assistant for qualification and follow-up.

Property Lead Advisor is built for practical commercial decisions: clearer qualification, stronger follow-up, better notes, and better judgment before time gets wasted on the wrong prospect.

It gives real estate professionals a clearer way to slow down, assess what is actually there, and choose the right next move with less guesswork across buyers, sellers, investors, and foreign buyers.

Problem

Not every lead deserves the same attention.

Some are ready. Some are unclear. Some sound promising but still hide weak motivation, poor timing, missing information, or friction you only notice too late.

Without a structured way to assess readiness and handle follow-up, it becomes easy to spend time on the wrong calls, the wrong visits, the wrong nurture cycle, and the wrong next commercial step.

What it does

A practical lead and follow-up assistant for everyday decisions.

Property Lead Advisor helps you review a lead before you commit time, attention, and follow-up effort.

  • Assess lead readiness
  • Identify likely blockers
  • See what information is still missing
  • Understand whether the lead is strong, weak, or still unclear
  • Decide the next best action
  • Generate a follow-up message you can actually send
  • Create short CRM-ready notes
  • Suggest nurture plans for longer sales cycles
  • Support investor and foreign buyer cases
  • Explain the typical next commercial step in Portugal

This is not a full CRM or a pipeline manager. It is a practical assistant for qualification, follow-up, and the next commercial step.

How it works

Simple in, useful out.

  1. Choose the lead type: buyer, seller, investor, or foreign buyer.
  2. Add the key lead context: motivation, timing, seriousness, financing, criteria, blockers, and only the commercial context that matters.
  3. Get a structured review showing lead strength, temperature, viability, what is missing, possible blockers, the best next action, and a ready-to-send follow-up message.
  4. When useful, also get a CRM-ready note, a nurture direction, buyer criteria guidance, or a simple Portugal process next step.

Who it is for

Built for real estate professionals who need better lead decisions.

  • Independent consultants
  • Small real estate teams
  • Agents handling multiple incoming leads
  • Professionals who want a clearer qualification process
  • Newer agents who need structure without complexity
  • Teams handling investors, relocation, or foreign buyer flows

Why it helps

A clearer lead process changes the quality of the work.

  • Focus attention where it matters
  • See faster when a lead is not ready yet
  • Spot friction earlier
  • Reduce wasted visits
  • Follow up with more confidence
  • Keep your next step clearer and faster
  • Handle longer sales cycles more consistently
